University Club - General Information

Welcome to our University Club!

About Us:
Our club is dedicated to bringing together students who share common interests and goals. We organize regular meetings, workshops, and social events throughout the academic year.

Meeting Schedule:
- Regular meetings: Every Wednesday at 5:00 PM in Room 204, Student Center
- Board meetings: First Monday of each month at 6:00 PM
- Social events: Last Friday of each month

Membership:
To become a member, you need to:
1. Be a current student at the university
2. Fill out the membership form on our website
3. Pay the annual membership fee of $20
4. Attend an orientation session

FAQ:
Q: Do I need to be a member to attend events?
A: Some events are open to all students, while others are members-only. Check the event details for specific information.

Q: Can I get a refund on my membership fee?
A: Membership fees are non-refundable, but they help support all our club activities.

Q: How do I propose a new event or initiative?
A: Bring your ideas to any meeting or email the board directly!
