Resources and Opportunities

Academic Resources:
- Tutoring sessions available every Tuesday and Thursday, 3-5 PM
- Study groups organized for major courses
- Access to past exam materials and study guides
- Resume review and career counseling services

Scholarships and Funding:
The club offers several scholarship opportunities:
1. Academic Excellence Scholarship: $1,000 awarded annually to members with GPA above 3.5
2. Leadership Award: $500 for members who demonstrate exceptional leadership
3. Project Grants: Up to $200 for member-initiated projects

To apply for scholarships:
- Application opens October 1st
- Deadline: November 15th
- Submit application via our website
- Include a personal statement and recommendation letter
- Winners announced in December
